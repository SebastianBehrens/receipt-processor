How Receipt Processing Works

1

Upload Receipts

Simply upload your receipt images as a ZIP file. Common formats like JPG, PNG are supported.

2

Extract Data

With the use of AI the receipts are scanned to extract purchased items and their prices from your receipts.

3

Sort & Assign

Manually assign each item to indicate who consumed it - because someone needs to take responsibility for that expensive cheese.

4

Calculate & Report

View total consumption amounts, calculate transfer payments between people, and see the total API costs for the processing.
